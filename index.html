Dipankar Mazumdar

Director of Developer Relations @ Cloudera

I lead worldwide developer initiatives focused on lakehouse and Generative AI currently at Cloudera. My focus is helping engineering teams build reliable & robust data platforms using open-source solutions like Apache Iceberg and Apache Hudi. My prior gigs were in roles such as Software and Data/ML Engineering. I completed my Masters Research on Explainable AI in 2021 from Dalhousie University, Canada.

I am the author of the book Engineering Lakehouses with Open Table Formats and have publicly spoken at numerous conferences such as Databricks’ Data+AI, Netflix Engineering, ApacheCon, Scale By the Bay, Data Day Texas, among others.
